Our Story

Crafted with Passion,
Served with Pride

Born in the vibrant heart of Kuwait City, LAYLA was founded on a single belief — that food is the language of love. Named after the world's most precious spice, we celebrate the rich culinary heritage of the Arabian Gulf.

Our team of master chefs blends traditional Kuwaiti recipes with contemporary techniques, creating dishes that honour the past while embracing the future. Every plate tells a story of culture, family, and extraordinary flavour.
