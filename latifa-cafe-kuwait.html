Our Story

A Place Built on
Warmth & Love

Latifa Cafe was born from a simple dream — to create a cozy corner in Kuwait where everyone feels welcome. Named after our founder's grandmother, Latifa, every detail of this cafe carries her spirit of warmth, generosity, and love.

From our hand-selected specialty coffee beans to our freshly baked pastries made each morning, we pour heart into everything we serve. This is not just a cafe — it is your second home.
